ABOUT ME

Cazenovia College I Keuka College

Greetings! I’m Parker Sage, a 20-year-old currently enrolled at Keuka College, charting my academic journey after the closure of my previous school, Cazenovia. While at Cazenovia, I was passionately pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Social Media Marketing.

However, circumstances led to a change in plans, and with the school’s closure, I found myself transitioning to Keuka College to pursue a BA in Art and Design with a minor in Visual Communication Design.

Despite the unexpected twists, my dedication to creativity and design remains unwavering. At Keuka, I’m immersing myself in a diverse range of artistic disciplines, such as Video, Photography, Graphic Design, and the Fine Arts. fueled by a desire to explore new avenues of expression. Feel free to connect with me—I’m always open to sharing experiences and forging new connections in the vibrant world of art and design!
